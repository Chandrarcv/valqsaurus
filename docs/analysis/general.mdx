## Variance Analysis

<br />
Variance Analysis helps analyze variances against a benchmark for specific periods for all the KPIs in the model.<br />

By default, you will see the below screen upon clicking on Variance Analysis under the Analyze tab.
  <Zoom>
<img alt="ValQ logo" src={useBaseUrl('/doc-images/analyze/analyze1.1.png')} /> 
  </Zoom>
<br /> <br />

The Analyze tab consists of two panels:

On top of the page, you will find a panel containing the features that help to configure the analysis report as per your needs.
  <Zoom>
<img alt="ValQ logo" src={useBaseUrl('/doc-images/analyze/analyze1.2.png')} />
  </Zoom>
<br /> <br />

The other panel shows the Variance Analysis report prepared based on the model that you have built and it's configuration.

  </Zoom>
