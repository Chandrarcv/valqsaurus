## Why use Plan?

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
Planning, budgeting and forecasting are the main elements of a company's performance management framework. 
ValQ provides a self-service solution and common platform - 'Plan' to execute and monitor these functions enabling real-time reporting & analytics.
</ div> <br />

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
Plan supports quick, accurate and reliable decision making to meet the strategic and
operational needs of your business. With ValQ's intelligent plan grid you can perform all areas of 
organizational planning including financial planning and analysis, sales planning,
supply chain planning, workforce planning and marketing planning.
</ div>

## Pre-requisite

At least one Data Series and a ValQ model is all you need to Plan

## What can I do with Plan?

The most common uses of the Plan tab are as follows:
 - Create a new plan from scratch (Zero-based Budgeting)
 - Create a new plan/budget based on already built model
 - Plan, Budget and Forecast based on Key Values Drivers
 - Update an existing plan by leveraging various allocation methods
 - Plan continuously by creating a rolling forecast 
 - Perform Time Series Forecasting
 - Use plan as input for Scenario analysis and Variance analysis



> **Note** <br />
Plan tab cannot access Scenarios from Simulate tab on ValQ
