In this section, you'll learn to access Plan tab and update an existing plan using the Lock/ Unlock features on ValQ 

## Access Plan 

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
Click on the Plan icon as highlighted in the image below to open the Plan tab. Before creating a new plan, ValQ allows you to Lock your model
on the Plan tab screen. Locking your model ensures the following:
</div> <br />

 - Converts the dynamic nodes in your model to nodes
 - The structure of your model, the nodes and configuration on it are fixed while you're planning

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
In this regard, ValQ gives you a warning message saying 'Any changes made in the model will re-calculate all the allocations in each series
and archive the previous steps'. Therefore, we recommend you to completely build your model before you begin to plan. 
</div> <br />

Once you are ready to Plan, click the Lock button.

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>
  <Zoom>
<img alt="ValQ logo" src={useBaseUrl('/doc-images/plan/plan1.png')} />
  </Zoom>
</div> <br />

> **Note**
<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
In case you want to make changes to your model while planning, you can unlock your model at any time.
Click <Link to={useBaseUrl('docs/planning/access#update-plan')}>here</Link> to learn to unlock your model.
</div> <br />

After locking the model, you will now be able to:
 - Name the series under Series Name text box
 - Choose the series to start with from the drop-down list
 - Select the period for which you want to plan from Periods option  

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
In the below example, we have named the series as New Series 1 (default name), selected Baseline as the series to start planning for Periods
Jan-Dec.
</div> <br />

  </Zoom>
</div> <br />

The below Figure shows the result on Plan screen. You can use the slider to view the rest of the periods. 
</div> <br />

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
